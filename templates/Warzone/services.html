Our CCTV Services

Site Survey & Consultation

Every property is unique, which is why we begin with a detailed on-site survey. Our team inspects your premises and identifies blind spots.

We then analyze potential security risks and provide expert consultation on the best camera positions, system type, and features required to deliver maximum coverage and reliability.

CCTV System Design & Planning

We design security systems tailored to your needs, whether it’s for a small home, office, retail shop, or large industrial facility.

Our experts create a structured plan that includes the right choice of cameras (IP, analog, or hybrid), recording devices, storage solutions, and monitoring systems to ensure total protection and scalability for future expansion.

CCTV Installation & Setup

Our professional installation service includes mounting cameras, wiring, connecting DVR/NVR systems, and configuring both local and remote access.

We ensure neat cabling, optimal positioning for clear visuals, and provide training so you can manage your CCTV system easily from your smartphone, tablet, or PC.

CCTV System Integration

Security works best when systems are connected. We integrate your CCTV with additional solutions like burglar alarms, fire alarms, motion sensors, intercoms, and access control systems.

This ensures a smart and unified security network where all devices work together seamlessly for your safety.

Web Design & Development

We create modern, responsive websites tailored to your business needs. From landing pages to full-scale e-commerce platforms, our designs are visually appealing and user-friendly.

Our team ensures your website is optimized for speed, mobile devices, and SEO, giving your business a professional online presence that attracts and retains customers.

Networking & Storage Solutions

Reliable storage is critical for CCTV. We set up secure storage options such as local DVR/NVR systems, high-capacity hard drives, and cloud backup for remote access.

Our networking solutions allow you to monitor multiple sites from one place, with smooth video streaming and easy retrieval of recorded footage whenever you need it.

Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC)

We offer affordable annual maintenance contracts that keep your CCTV systems running without interruptions. This includes routine inspections, lens cleaning, and performance checks.

We also provide software updates and on-demand repairs. With AMC, you get peace of mind knowing your security system is always in top condition and ready to protect.
